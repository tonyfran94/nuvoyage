African Safari Adventure Package

  • Destination: Nairobi & Maasai Mara, Kenya
  • Duration: 7 Days / 6 Nights
  • Flight: Round-trip economy flight from New York (JFK) to Nairobi (NBO) with Kenya Airways
  • Hotel: 3 nights at Hemingways Nairobi (5-star luxury hotel), 3 nights at Mara Serena Safari Lodge
  • Transportation: Airport transfers, private safari vehicle, domestic flight Nairobi-Maasai Mara
  • Highlights: Safari game drives, Maasai village visit, Nairobi National Park tour, all meals included, optional hot air balloon safari

Asian Cultural & Beach Escape

  • Destination: Bangkok & Phuket, Thailand
  • Duration: 8 Days / 7 Nights
  • Flight: Round-trip economy flight from London (LHR) to Bangkok (BKK) with Thai Airways
  • Hotel: 3 nights at Mandarin Hotel Bangkok, 4 nights at The Shore at Katathani, Phuket
  • Transportation: Airport transfers, private city tour, domestic flight Bangkok-Phuket, beach transfers

European Historic & Romantic Escape

  • Destination: Paris & Rome
  • Duration: 9 Days / 8 Nights
  • Flight: Round-trip economy flight from Los Angeles (LAX) to Paris (CDG) via Delta Airlines
  • Hotel: 4 nights at Hotel Le Meurice, Paris; 4 nights at Hotel Artemide, Rome
  • Transportation: Airport transfers, TGV train Paris-Rome, private guided tours, optional dinner cruises
  • Highlights: Eiffel Tower, Louvre, Seine cruise, Colosseum, Vatican Museums, fine dining, shopping

Rodrigues Island Escape

  • Destination: Rodrigues Island, Mauritius
  • Duration: 6 Days / 5 Nights
  • Flight: Round-trip flight from Mauritius (MRU) to Rodrigues (RRG) with Air Mauritius
  • Hotel: 5 nights at Tekoma Boutik Hotel (beachfront boutique)
  • Transportation: Airport transfers, island tour, boat trip to Île aux Cocos
  • Highlights: Snorkeling, hiking, local cuisine, traditional music, lagoon excursions, authentic island culture

About Us

At NuVoyage by Eco Crew, we believe travel is more than a destination — it’s a soulful experience that connects people, cultures, and nature. Our mission is to craft personalized, meaningful journeys that awaken the spirit and leave a lasting impact on both travelers and host communities. Behind NuVoyage is Sweety Moonian Francois, a dedicated travel curator with over a decade of experience guiding travelers across continents. Born with an adventurous heart and a love for connecting people with purpose, Sweety has worked closely with guests from around the world, speaking fluent English, French, Creole, and Italian. Her deep understanding of cultural nuance and her warm, professional approach make each journey feel seamless and inspired. From the bustling streets of Bangkok to the wild savannas of Kenya, and the romantic alleys of Paris and Rome, Sweety tailors every experience to match your dreams — with care, comfort, and authenticity. Whether you’re seeking an African safari, a beach escape in Asia, or a historic European romance, she ensures every detail reflects your unique spirit. Travel with NuVoyage, and let your story unfold beautifully — wherever the world takes you.
